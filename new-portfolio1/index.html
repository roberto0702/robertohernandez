Creative Designer based in San Jose, California

Roberto Hernandez is a creative designer, specializing in brand identity and digital experience design. With a background in graphic design, advertising and coding, my work explores themes of memory, identity, and technology. As I continue to push boundaries in the creative space. I’m passionate about storytelling, typography, and creating impactful brand experiences. I aim to connect people through my storytelling and visuals. No matter what type of business you have, establishing a strong brand identity is key to connecting with your audience.
As an artist, I am captivated by the unseen forces that shape our daily lives—emotions, memories, and the passage of time. We are all creative! It can be in photography, coding, fashion, videography, or drawing. Creativity is valuable in various fields, including the arts, sciences, business, and everyday life. It fosters innovation, problem-solving, and the development of new and improved ideas and products. Cultivating a creative mindset involves encouraging curiosity, embracing challenges, and maintaining an open and exploratory approach to the world.
